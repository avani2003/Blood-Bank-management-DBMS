Who we are and what we do?

We are a government led blood bank who conduct blood donation drives around Dubai. Blood banks need to keep track of the blood units they have in inventory, as well as information about the donors who donated the blood. A database allows for efficient record keeping, making it easier to track inventory levels, donor information, and other important data.
